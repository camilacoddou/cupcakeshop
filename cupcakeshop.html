Welcome to I'll Take You to the Cup Cake Shop!

Owned and operated by Seamus Shalman and Camila Coddou, bringing you all the best in queer cup cakes.

Here is our story:

Seamus and Camila met a decade ago at a cupcake convention. They both love cupcakes so much but didn't feel there were enough queer cupcake flavors. So they started their own cupcake shop.

Cupcakes Are:

  • Delicious
  • Not Good For You!
